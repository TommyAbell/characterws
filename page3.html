Popular Culture

Joker Facts ?

The Joker is one of the most iconic characters in popular culture. He is in the list of the greatest comic book characters ever created. He has been in many merchandise, such as clothing and collectible items. The Joker has been in many live action and animated films. He is also apart of the popular Batman Arkham video game series. Cesar Romero has played Joker in the 1960's Batman television series. Jack Nicholson in Batman (1989) and Heath Ledger in The Dark Knight (2008) have also played Joker. Joaquin Phoenix plays Joker in the film Joker in 2019. Mark Hamill, Troy Baker, and others have given voice to the character.
